- openssl genrsa -out server.key 1024 [生成服务器私钥]
- openssl req -new -key server.key -out server.csr [根据私钥和输入的信息(域名)生成证书请求文件]
- openssl x509 -req -in server.csr -out server.crt -signkey server.key -days 3650 [用私钥和证书请求文件生成证书]
